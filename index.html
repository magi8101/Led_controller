Frequently Asked Questions

What hardware do I need to use this app? +

You'll need an Android device with Bluetooth capability and a compatible Bluetooth module (like HC-05 or HC-06) connected to your LED circuit. The app works with most Arduino and ESP32/ESP8266 based projects.

Is the app compatible with RGB LED strips? +

Yes! The app supports both single-color LEDs and RGB LED strips. For RGB strips, you'll have access to color selection and various lighting effects.

What's the maximum range for the Bluetooth connection? +

The typical range for Bluetooth connections is about 10 meters (33 feet), but this can vary depending on environmental factors and obstacles between your device and the Bluetooth module.

Does the app require an internet connection? +

No, the app works completely offline. It communicates directly with your Bluetooth module without needing an internet connection.
